Sumo Bot

The purpose of the SumoBot was to apply the theory and proper engineering skills to construct a robot that has the ability to sense another robot and push it out of a ring. Softwares used include TraxMaker 3 pro and MicroCode Studio.

Many skills were used in the designing and construction of this robot. Knowledge of motors, bot detection, and line detection were further explored throughout the duration of this project.
